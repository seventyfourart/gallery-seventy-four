About

A practice shaped by curiosity and quiet devotion

Welcome to my online gallery. I'm Anuradha, a visual artist with a long-standing passion for watercolor painting. For many years, I focused exclusively on watercolors, exploring the unpredictable fluidity and spontaneity that the medium offers.

Although I had been sewing small projects before 2025, I was introduced to the rhythm, geometry, and interplay of pattern, texture, and color that quilting allows, and it has become my current artistic focus. My latest quilting work is showcased below along with some short excursions into other sewing projects.

My artistic journey has always unfolded through themes that shift with the seasons and stages of life. I maintain a daily creative practice that keeps my skills and ideas evolving. Many of my earlier works emerged from what I call "delayed urban sketches" — cityscapes reimagined from photographs after the moment has passed.

I welcome commission inquiries and enjoy collaborating on custom pieces. Follow my evolving journey here and on Instagram.
